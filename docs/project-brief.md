# KYIRA ENERGIES - Project Brief

## Project

KYIRA ENERGIES is a professional B2B website project for a company in the energy sector.

The website is a one-page landing page designed to present the company, its expertise, its services, its support method, and to encourage contact requests.

## Business context

Client: KYIRA ENERGIES
Contact: Yannis Miame
Sector: energy, energy optimization, tertiary buildings, energy savings, decarbonization
Website type: professional institutional landing page
Main goal: generate contact requests from B2B prospects

## Target audience

The website targets professionals and organizations in the tertiary sector, such as:

- companies
- office buildings
- commercial premises
- hotels and hospitality
- public buildings
- local authorities

These sectors must remain generic unless confirmed by the client.

## Main conversion goal

The main CTA sends users to the contact form.

Secondary contact options may include:

- public phone number
- public email address

These contact details must remain placeholders until confirmed by the client.

## Content rules

Do not invent facts about KYIRA ENERGIES.

Do not invent:

- certifications
- client references
- results
- percentages
- case studies
- partners
- awards
- years of experience
- legal claims
- technical guarantees

Unknown information must use clear placeholders such as:

- To be confirmed
- Certification to confirm with KYIRA ENERGIES
- Client references to be added later
- Phone number to confirm
- Email address to confirm

## Tone

The tone must be:

- serious
- clear
- professional
- B2B
- trustworthy
- simple to understand
- not too technical
- not too startup-like

The website must inspire trust quickly and avoid exaggerated marketing language.

## UX/UI direction

The design should be:

- sober
- premium
- corporate
- modern
- responsive
- accessible
- easy to scan

Avoid:

- excessive animations
- fake metrics
- flashy gradients
- complex layouts
- overly playful visuals
- generic buzzwords
